CFD — Turbulent Flow Over a Backward-Facing Step

Steady-state RANS simulation in ANSYS Fluent at Re = 5100, Er = 1.2, using the SST k-ω turbulence model. Validated against DNS data (Le et al., 1997). Grid independence study across five meshes; reattachment length Xr = 6.53h vs DNS 6.28h (4% error). TKE overprediction of 230% at x/h = 6 quantified and explained. 9th-order polynomial curve fit applied to velocity profiles (R² = 1.000).

FEA — Planar Truss & Belleville Spring Washer

Two-part FEA study. Part 1: 8-member planar truss solved via MATLAB LU decomposition (8×8 system), verified in ANSYS Mechanical — max nodal displacement error 0.006%. Part 2: Belleville spring constant evaluated analytically via central finite difference (k = 12,956 N/mm) and by 2D axisymmetric vs 3D FEA. Critical finding: 3D model overestimated stiffness by 58.7% due to over-constrained boundary conditions.

Truss error: 0.006% · Spring k analytical: 12,956 N/mm · 2D FEA error: 2.42%

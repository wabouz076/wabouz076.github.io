SolidWorks Commercial Aircraft ·
Full Assembly

Full commercial aircraft assembly modelled in SolidWorks. The model includes a twin turbofan engine arrangement, retractable tricycle landing gear, detailed fuselage with cabin windows and door cutouts, swept wing geometry, and a T-tail empennage. All components are fully mated within the top-level assembly.
